📘 About the Portal

The Community Event Portal is designed to connect citizens with local activities, government services, and cultural programs in an easy and interactive way.

This platform allows residents to register for events, check schedules, view locations, and provide feedback instantly.

Our goal is to build a stronger community by encouraging participation in:

  • 🎵 Cultural and Music Events
  • 🏥 Health Awareness Camps
  • ⚽ Sports and Fitness Activities
  • 🎓 Educational Workshops
  • 🌳 Environmental Clean-up Drives

This portal is developed using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to demonstrate modern web development practices and responsive design principles.
